Tutorial: How To Use

Note: Instagram should be opened on a website as you cannot request your data on the mobile application.

  1. Step 1: Login to the Instagram account that you want to check with.
  2. Step 2: Navigate towards the "Your activity" tab in "More".
  3. Step 3: Request data download in "Download your information" tab.
  4. Step 4: Click on "Continue" button, then proceed to "Request a download".
  5. Step 5: Click on "Download or transfer information" button, then select the account you want to retrieve in, and then select "Next".
  6. Step 6: Select "All available information", then "Download to device".
  7. Step 7: Have "Data range" selected to "All time" and save, then "Format" selected to "JSON", and save.
  8. Step 8: Click on "Create files" button, and wait to recieve data through the email that you've notified.
  9. Step 9: Download the file from the email sent to you by Instagram, and extract all folders from that zip file.
  10. Step 10: Navigate towards the Instagram Follower Tracker page.
  11. Step 11: Select the followers and following file from the extracted folder named "followers_and_following", directory in the "connections" folder.
  12. Step 12: Click the "Submit" button, and then review the results.

This tutorial applies for both PC and mobile.
